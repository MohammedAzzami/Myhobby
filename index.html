My Favorite Hobby is Playing Video Games

Reasons why I play video Games:

  • Gray matter in the brain:
    1. Gaming is really a workout for your mind disguised as fun.
    2. Increase gray matter in the brain and boost brain connectivity.

  • Better Social Skills:
    1. More likely to have good social skills and perform better academically.
    2. Build better relationships with others.

  • Better Problem Solver:
    1. Learning to think on your feet.
    2. Multi-level games are designed like complex puzzles that take several hours to solve.

  • Inspire you to be more persistent:
    1. You either win or you keep trying, learning from your mistakes.
    2. Become more confident and to work towards your goals.
